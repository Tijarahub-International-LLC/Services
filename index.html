Verification: Submit required documentation for verification, including business licenses and tax information. Setup: Complete your seller profile, including uploading product listings, setting prices, and defining shipping options. Training: Participate in onboarding sessions and review training materials to familiarize yourself with the platform. Launch: Once approved, your products will go live, and you can start receiving orders.

    Tijarahub offers flexible logistics and shipping solutions to streamline the process: You can choose from various shipping partners integrated into the platform for efficient delivery such as FedEx, UPS, and DHL.

    Sellers can monitor their performance through: Dashboard Analytics: Access a comprehensive dashboard with real-time sales data, including total sales, order volume, and revenue.

    TijaraHub prioritizes the security of transactions on its platform. We employ robust security measures and encryption protocols and regularly update our systems to safeguard user data and ensure secure business operations.
